# AKE Parameters

The AKE index integrates nine physically independent parameters:

## 1. KED - Kinetic Energy Density (22%)

**Formula:** `KED = ½ρv³ [W/m²]`

The fundamental currency of wind energy. Captures available power flux.

## 2. TII - Turbulence Intensity Index (16%)

**Formula:** `TII = σᵥ/v̄`

Flow instability measure affecting fatigue loading.

## 3. VSR - Vertical Shear Ratio (14%)

**Formula:** `v(z)/v(z_ref) = (z/z_ref)^α`

Wind speed gradient with altitude, critical for large rotors.

## 4. AOD - Aerosol Optical Depth (12%)

Particulate loading affecting solar potential and gust formation.

## 5. THD - Thermal Helicity Dynamics (10%)

**Formula:** `∫ω·∇T dV`

Rotational kinetic energy from thermal gradients, key for gust prediction.

## 6. PGF - Pressure Gradient Force (8%)

**Formula:** `−(1/ρ)∇p`

Primary kinematic driver of atmospheric motion.

## 7. HCI - Humidity-Convection Interaction (7%)

Latent heat release in convective updrafts.

## 8. ASI - Atmospheric Stability Integration (6%)

Richardson number through troposphere.

## 9. LRC - Local Roughness Coefficient (5%)

Terrain-derived roughness length z₀.
